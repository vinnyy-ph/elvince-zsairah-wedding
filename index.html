Our love story

2008

Opposites Attract

It was a high school classic: Elvince was the tall, silent type , while Zsairah was the short, bubbly and friendly girl who lit up the room. He towered over her, but she was the one who captured his attention.

After Elvince's crush was spilled by a friend, three chaotic days of matchmaking, teasing, and conversation followed. On their last subject, while walking the corridor, the silent type executed a brave plan: he quickly pulled Zsairah's bag behind, stopping her, and in a moment of courage slipped his arm around her shoulders. Looking down at the girl who held his heart, he asked: "Zsai, mahal kita... mahal mo ba ako?" Then, he added, "Bukas ang sagot ha". Zsairah pulled her bag back and took off running, both hearts were throbbing with excitement and nerves. The walk home was an awkward, electric silence — until the very next day, November 26, 2008, she said yes, and the love story officially began.

2009–2012

The Math Partners

The high school years weren't just about stolen glances; they were about shared dedication and growth. As dedicated math competition partners, they trained side-by-side. Zsairah, the smartest girl in the class, graduated as the Salutatorian, keeping their young love focused squarely on academics. Their relationship was constantly supervised — especially since Elvince's mother taught at the school — but it flourished under accepted guidance.

2012–2013

StarLove

Their young love took a necessary pause in 2012 when Zsairah dedicated herself entirely to her role as a church youth leader. After months of silence, they met to officially close their chapter, but destiny had intervened. Under the bustling city night, Elvince glanced upward and saw a star that shines so bright, and spontaneously whispered the words that changed everything: "Ang ganda ng star oh." Zsairah instantly broke down, tears streaming. This was the exact celestial sign she had requested in prayer: the one who would truly appreciate the night sky with her would be her forever. Their chapter wasn't closed—it was beginning anew, committed to balancing faith, studies, and a love written in the stars.

2013–2019

The Adult Chapter

Their commitment, renewed under the watchful stars, quickly matured into a powerful partnership. Supporting each other through college, they learned to balance their studies, faith, and relationship. They both graduated college and built their careers, trading late-night study sessions for weekly dates and travels funded by their "adult money." They dedicated themselves not only to their shared path but to fostering each other's individual dreams, proving that true partnership means growing separately, yet always growing together for their future.

2019–2023

The Test of Time & Our Little Family

The pandemic led to a sudden move-in, which tested their partnership. As their love grew, so did their life, eventually welcoming their furbaby, Luna. Through apartment changes and shared daily life, the high school sweethearts cemented their bond. Everything was finally making beautiful sense.

2023

15 Years Later: The Question

On their 15th anniversary, Elvince set the stage for a casual, late-night deep talk, leading to the perfect timing to ask the question. With AirPods in and eyes closed, Zsairah listened to a song that perfectly captured the silent story of his heart. With fifteen years of shared love, he finally spoke the words he had always dreamed of: "Papoy, Will you marry me?" Zsairah said yes through happy tears, immediately punctuated by, "Ang pangit ng nails ko!" Their beloved furbaby, Luna, jumped right into them, making the promise official for their little family.

2026

The Forever Chapter

Seventeen years ago, our high school love story began. Now, that amazing chapter reaches its sweetest culmination as we joyfully say 'I do' and start our forever as husband and wife.

Celebrate with us as we officially tie the knot on February 4, 2026!

Is the ceremony and reception in the same place ?

Yes, the ceremony and reception will be held at Flor's Garden Antipolo.

Directions to Flors Garden

  1. Start on Marcos Highway: Begin your journey on Marcos Highway, heading toward Rizal.
  2. Drive Straight: Continue straight along Marcos Highway until you reach Boso-Boso Highlands. Look for signs pointing to various attractions in the area.
  3. Locate the Prayer Mountain Sign: As you approach Boso-Boso Highlands, watch for the sign indicating Prayer Mountain.
  4. Turn Right: Upon spotting the Prayer Mountain sign, make a right turn.
  5. Follow the Road: Proceed along the road after turning right. Signs will guide you to Flors Garden.

It's advisable to check traffic updates on your chosen navigation app before heading out to avoid any delays.
