Other Details

Is the ceremony and reception in the same place ?

Yes, the ceremony and reception will be held at Flor's Garden Antipolo.

Directions to Flors Garden

  1. Start on Marcos Highway: Begin your journey on Marcos Highway, heading toward Rizal.
  2. Drive Straight: Continue straight along Marcos Highway until you reach Boso-Boso Highlands. Look for signs pointing to various attractions in the area.
  3. Locate the Prayer Mountain Sign: As you approach Boso-Boso Highlands, watch for the sign indicating Prayer Mountain.
  4. Turn Right: Upon spotting the Prayer Mountain sign, make a right turn.
  5. Follow the Road: Proceed along the road after turning right. Signs will guide you to Flors Garden.

It's advisable to check traffic updates on your chosen navigation app before heading out to avoid any delays.

Alternatively, click this WAZE link or google maps link to find us.

Is there a parking space provided ?

Yes, free parking is available for all guests. We encourage our wedding guests to be at the venue 30 mins before the ceremony time.

What should I wear ?

We kindly request that our guests adhere to the wedding's color palette when selecting their attire. Our color scheme centers around luxurious neutrals such as Gold, Champagne, Beige, Apricot and Khaki. We encourage you to wear colors in this beautiful, soft range to enhance the overall aesthetic of our celebration. Please don't wear other colors especially black.

Can I take photos during the ceremony ?

No Phones, Please! We kindly ask that the ceremony remain unplugged. Our photographers and videographers have everything covered, and we want you—our dearest friends and family—to relax, be present, and enjoy this time with us through your eyes. Once the reception begins, please feel free to take as many photos as you like!

What to do I do if I'm late to the ceremony ?

If you are late to the ceremony, please do not walk down the aisle and try to find a seat. While we want everyone to be there, we would appreciate if you ask assistance from our coordinator or wait to join us until the ceremony ends.

Can I sit anywhere during the reception ?

We planned a seating arrangement for our guest's. Our coordinator will gladly assist you on your designated seats.

How can I help the couple on their wedding day ?

Be there to celebrate with us. Wear appropriate attire. Stay with us til the end of the program. Have fun! PS: Prepare a little message for us
